Why Rehearsal?

Conversation, not delegation.

Case studies give students facts. LocoEnsayo gives them conversations. The AI characters don't hand over answers — they play their roles, and students have to do the professional work of asking, listening, and analysing.

🗣️

Active, Not Passive

Students don't read about stakeholders. They talk to them. They ask follow-up questions. They deal with inconsistencies between what different people say. That's the real skill.

🔒

Privacy by Design

All AI inference runs locally via Ollama. No student data leaves the institution. No cloud APIs. No tracking. Full institutional control over the infrastructure.

🎓

Mirrors Real Practice

In real projects, the hardest part is understanding the people and the organisation. LocoEnsayo lets students practise exactly that — in a safe, repeatable environment.

📈

Assess the Analysis

What matters is what students do with the information, not the raw transcripts. Assess their professional judgement, their recommendations, their ability to synthesise conflicting input.

How It Works

Simple architecture. Rich interactions.

Each AI character is a fine-tuned small language model served locally. Students chat through a web interface. Everything runs on-premises.

1

Fine-Tuned Characters

Each AI employee is a small language model fine-tuned with a specific backstory, personality, knowledge set, and conversational style. They stay in character.

2

Local Inference

Models run locally via Ollama. No cloud. No API keys. No student data leaves the institution. BYOK options available for educators who prefer commercial models.

3

Web Chat Interface

Students interact through a familiar chat interface. Pick an employee, start a conversation. The AI remembers context within the session.
