## What is LocoEnsayo?

**"Ensayo"** means *rehearsal* in Spanish. LocoEnsayo is a platform for AI-driven teaching scenarios where students interact with AI-populated simulated organisations. Think of it as a rehearsal stage where the cast is made up of AI characters, each with their own role, personality, and backstory.

Students don't just read case studies — they **have conversations** with the people inside them.

## How It Works

Each scenario is a simulated organisation with multiple AI chatbot "employees." Students interact with these characters to practise real-world professional skills:

- **Security audits** — interview staff to identify vulnerabilities
- **Requirements gathering** — talk to stakeholders to understand business needs
- **Business analysis** — explore organisational challenges through conversation

The AI characters are powered by fine-tuned small language models running locally via [Ollama](https://ollama.ai), ensuring complete privacy.

## Conversation, Not Delegation

LocoEnsayo follows a **"Conversation not Delegation"** philosophy. The AI characters are not there to do the work for the student — they're there to be interviewed, questioned, and engaged with. Students must bring their own professional judgement. The AI provides the context; the student provides the analysis.

This mirrors real-world practice, where the hardest part of any project is understanding the people and the organisation, not writing the report.

## Privacy First

All inference runs locally. No student data leaves the institution. No cloud APIs. No tracking. This matters for:

- **Student privacy** — conversations stay on-premises
- **Academic integrity** — the AI plays a role, it doesn't write assignments
- **Institutional control** — universities own their infrastructure

## Part of LocoLab

LocoEnsayo is a project of [LocoLab](https://locolabo.org), an applied AI research lab at Curtin University's School of Management and Marketing. LocoLab explores practical, accessible AI for education — from fine-tuned local models ([LocoLLM](https://locollm.org)) to simulated teaching environments.

## AI Development Toolkit — Developer Rehearsal

Students learn to build AI applications through progressive Python packages. A different kind of rehearsal: practising *building* AI systems rather than *interacting* with them.

| Package | What Students Learn | Install |
|---------|-------------------|---------|
| [SimpleBot](/docs/simplebot/) | First LLM interaction — one function, one response | `pip install simplebot` |
| [HandsOnAI](/docs/hands-on-ai/) | Chat, RAG, and agent patterns — the three pillars of AI applications | `pip install hands-on-ai` |
| [SimLab](/docs/sim-lab/) | Complex systems modelling — business, ecological, and statistical simulations | `pip install sim-lab` |
